What I Do

I'm an Associate Project Engineer at Oates Associates, Inc. in St. Louis, working in transportation civil engineering — the discipline focused on the infrastructure that moves people: roads, highways, intersections, bridges, and the systems that connect them.

Before returning to Oates, I spent two years as a Designer at Benesch in Boston, deepening my experience across a range of transportation projects. The through-line across both firms is the same: applied problem-solving within real constraints, producing work that gets built.

A design lives on paper for months or years before it becomes concrete and steel. Getting it right matters — these are systems people depend on every day without noticing them. That invisibility is the goal.

I'm an EIT and have passed the PE exam — license pending.
