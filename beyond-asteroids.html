Asteroids

Overview

A modern take on the Asteroids formula — the rotational flight of the original, extended with new content and mechanics. Navigate asteroid fields, engage planet-side defenses, and dock at a fully realized space station hub between runs.

Built out of genuine affection for the genre. A first project that does what first projects should: get something real and playable into the world, then keep building on it.
