About

I'm a civil engineer working in transportation — roads, bridges, and infrastructure systems that people use every day without thinking about them. That invisibility is the point. Good engineering disappears into the world.

Outside of work, I build indie games, make digital art, and write fantasy fiction. Different disciplines, the same underlying drive — making things that didn't exist before.

This site collects both sides. The professional and the personal are here because they belong to the same person.
