About the Work

Abbora and Abbir — twin cities born of the same blood, founded by brothers who chose rivalry over ruin. For generations the wound held. Now it has broken open, and the war between them threatens to swallow the known world.

At the center of it is Titus Victus Hamiota — Victor, to the men who follow him. Trident of a Serpent traces his path through the conflict, but the world he moves through is not quite the one any historian would recognize.

The myths were always real. The gods were always watching. Whether they were on anyone's side is a different question.
