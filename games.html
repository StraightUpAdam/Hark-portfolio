The Studio

CUBED Pluto is the name under which I make games. It's a one-person operation — design, programming, art, and sound all handled by the same set of hands.

The games tend toward the arcade and action side: simple rules, deep execution, the kind of thing that rewards a second and third attempt. Classic games updated for modern sensibilities, or original ideas that share that same tight feedback loop.

Everything is built in Unity. Development happens alongside a full-time engineering career, which means projects move at their own pace — but they do move.
